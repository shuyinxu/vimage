Tutorial

Play the Game!

Using your Vivid Imagination with Vimage

1. Select a category of subjects to draw on the home page.

2. Upon loading the canvas, Player 1 must draw their given subject as fast as they can within the time limit.

3. The timer will go off to prompt Player 2 to start.

4. Player 2 must then transform the previous drawing into their own assigned subject, which will also be within the category.

5. To clear your canvas, press the "Clear" button at the bottom of your canvas.

Extra Features

- To end the game, click "End Game" on the right side of the page.

- Save your beautiful drawing, press the "S" key on your keyboard.

- If you do not want to play game mode, choose the game mode, 'FREE PLAY', and you can draw anything you want for as long as you heart desires!
