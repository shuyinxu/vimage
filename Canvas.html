Instructions

In this game you will test your vivid imagination and creative abilities. Once you start drawing you will get a subject and a time limit. Try to complete the drawing within the time limit. The drawing will pass on to player 2 regardless if finished or not.To save your beautiful drawing press the s key on your keyboard.We hope that this game will help you to share,influence, and inspire creativity around the world.
